## Question 1: Words in Context (Science)

**Passage:**
Marine biologists studying coral reef ecosystems have observed that certain species of parrotfish play a crucial role in maintaining reef health. By grazing on algae that would otherwise smother coral, these fish help preserve the delicate balance of the ecosystem. This symbiotic relationship demonstrates how individual species can be ______ to the overall functioning of their habitats.

**Stem:**
Which choice completes the text with the most logical and precise word or phrase?

**Options:**
A) irrelevant  
B) detrimental  
C) peripheral  
D) essential

**Correct Answer:** D) essential

## Question 2: Text Structure (Science/Environment)

**Passage:**
In the mid-20th century, urban planners in many American cities prioritized automobile traffic over pedestrian accessibility, designing wide streets and minimizing sidewalks. Decades later, these decisions have been linked to reduced physical activity and increased air pollution in urban centers. In response, some cities have begun implementing "road diets," reducing vehicle lanes to create space for bike paths and wider sidewalks. Early studies suggest these modifications have improved both air quality and public health metrics in participating communities.

**Stem:**
Which choice best describes the overall structure of the text?

**Options:**
A) It summarizes a historical problem and then describes a contemporary response to that problem.  
B) It introduces a current debate among urban planners and then presents evidence supporting one side.  
C) It evaluates the effectiveness of various transportation policies and then recommends a specific approach.  
D) It explains why early urban planners favored automobiles and then defends their decision-making process.

**Correct Answer:** A) It summarizes a historical problem and then describes a contemporary response to that problem.

## Question 3: Grammar - Subject-Verb Agreement (Science)

**Passage:**
The diverse community of microorganisms that inhabit the human gut, collectively known as the gut microbiome, ______ increasingly recognized as essential to digestive health and immune function.

**Stem:**
Which choice completes the text so that it conforms to the conventions of Standard English?

**Options:**
A) are  
B) have been  
C) were  
D) is

**Correct Answer:** D) is

## Question 4: Data Interpretation - Graph (Social Studies)

**Passage:**
The following graph shows the percentage of renewable energy in total electricity generation for four countries from 2010 to 2020.

```
Renewable Energy as Percentage of Electricity Generation

  60% |                                        ____
      |                                       /
  50% |                                      /
      |                                     /
  40% |                            ____----
      |                           /
  30% |          ____-------------
      |         /
  20% |        /
      |       /
  10% |------
      |
    0% +------------------------------------
         2010    2012    2014    2016    2018    2020

      —— Country A (solid line)
      - - - Country B (dashed line)
      · · · Country C (dotted line)
      — — Country D (dash-dot line)
```

**Key:** Country A = solid, Country B = dashed, Country C = dotted, Country D = dash-dot

Based on the data in the graph, which of the following statements is best supported?

**Options:**
A) By 2020, Country A had the highest percentage of renewable energy in its electricity mix.  
B) Country C showed the most consistent increase in renewable energy percentage throughout the decade.  
C) In 2015, Countries B and C had approximately the same percentage of renewable energy.  
D) All four countries achieved at least 30% renewable energy by 2020.

**Correct Answer:** A) By 2020, Country A had the highest percentage of renewable energy in its electricity mix.
