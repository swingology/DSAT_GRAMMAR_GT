# Generated DSAT Practice Questions - Set 2 (More Competitive Distractors)

## Question 1: Words in Context (Science) - HIGH COMPETITION

**Passage:**
Climate scientists have observed that Arctic sea ice is declining at an accelerating rate. While seasonal fluctuations are normal, the extent of ice loss observed since the 1980s exceeds historical patterns. This trend suggests that current warming conditions are ______ what natural variability alone would predict.

**Stem:**
Which choice completes the text with the most logical and precise word or phrase?

**Options:**
A) consistent with  
B) altering  
C) outpacing  
D) comparable to

**Correct Answer:** C) outpacing

---

## Question 2: Text Structure (History/Literature) - HIGH COMPETITION

**Passage:**
In her 1851 novel *The House of the Seven Gables*, Nathaniel Hawthorne's ancestor John Hathorne is never named directly, yet his presence haunts the narrative. The fictional Pyncheon family inherits wealth built upon the wrongful accusation of Matthew Maule, a parallel to Hathorne's role as a judge in the Salem witch trials. Hawthorne thus explores how ancestral guilt reverberates through generations while simultaneously confronting his own family's complicated legacy.

**Stem:**
Which choice best describes the overall structure of the text?

**Options:**
A) It introduces a historical fact about an author's ancestor and then explains how that fact influenced a specific literary work.
B) It describes a plot element in a novel and then traces that element's connection to the author's personal history.
C) It compares a fictional family's actions to a historical event and then suggests a reason for that comparison's significance.
D) It summarizes a novel's central theme and then evaluates how effectively the author develops that theme.

**Correct Answer:** B) It describes a plot element in a novel and then traces that element's connection to the author's personal history.

## Question 3: Grammar - Subject-Verb Agreement (Science) - HIGH TRAP INTENSITY

**Passage:**
The collection of proteins that facilitate chemical reactions in living cells, known collectively as enzymes, ______ essential for processes ranging from digestion to DNA replication.

**Stem:**
Which choice completes the text so that it conforms to the conventions of Standard English?

**Options:**
A) are  
B) is  
C) have been  
D) were

**Correct Answer:** B) is

## Question 4: Data Interpretation - Table (Science) - HIGH COMPETITION

**Passage:**
The following table shows the average growth rates (in centimeters per year) of three tree species under different irrigation conditions over a five-year study.

| Tree Species | Low Irrigation | Moderate Irrigation | High Irrigation |
|------------|----------------|---------------------|-----------------|
| Oak        | 12.5           | 24.0                | 22.0            |
| Pine       | 8.0            | 18.5                | 19.5            |
| Maple      | 15.0           | 21.0                | 20.5            |

**Stem:**
Based on the data in the table, which of the following conclusions is most strongly supported?

**Options:**
A) Increasing irrigation always results in higher growth rates for all tree species.
B) Oak trees benefit more from moderate irrigation than from either low or high irrigation.
C) Maple trees showed the highest growth rate under low irrigation conditions compared to other species.
D) Pine trees experienced their greatest growth improvement when moving from low to moderate irrigation.

**Correct Answer:** D) Pine trees experienced their greatest growth improvement when moving from low to moderate irrigation.
