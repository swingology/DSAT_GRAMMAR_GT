### Question 1

Which choice completes the text with the most logical and precise word or phrase?

**A)** functional

**B)** rudimentary

**C)** ornamental

**D)** utilitarian

**Correct Answer: B**

**Explanation:** The passage emphasizes that the water systems were "far more extensive than previously understood" and "far from being merely" something simple, suggesting the need for a word indicating basic or primitive construction. "Rudimentary" (meaning basic or elementary) fits this contrast—if they were far from rudimentary, they must have been sophisticated. The other options don't create this necessary contrast: "functional" and "utilitarian" would be consistent with the sophisticated description, while "ornamental" introduces a decorative element never mentioned.
