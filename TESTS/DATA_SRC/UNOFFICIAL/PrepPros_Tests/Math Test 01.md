**Question 1** *(Multiple Choice)*

A certain university offers courses that are either 3 credits or 4 credits per course. Each semester, a student registered for a total of 16 credits for the fall semester. Which equation represents the possible numbers of 3-credit courses **x** and 4-credit courses **y** that the student could have registered for?

- A) 3x + y = 16
- B) 3x + 4y = 16
- C) 4x + 3y = 16
- D) x + y = 16
