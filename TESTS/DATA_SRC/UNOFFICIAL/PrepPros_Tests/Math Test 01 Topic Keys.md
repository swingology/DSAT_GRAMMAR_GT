## Key Tips & Strategies Mentioned in the Video
- To find a **y-intercept**, always plug in x = 0
- For **perpendicular slopes**, flip the sign and the fraction (negative reciprocal)
- For **similar figures**, area scales by the square of the linear scale factor
- For **absolute value equations**, always isolate the absolute value first, then split into ± cases
- For **systems of equations**, convert to slope-intercept form to compare slopes and intercepts
- Use **substitution with real numbers** to verify answers on geometry scaling questions
- Always check what the question is **actually asking for** — e.g., 2x vs. x (a common trap on Q19)
- For **vertex form parabolas**, verify by plugging in the vertex coordinates
