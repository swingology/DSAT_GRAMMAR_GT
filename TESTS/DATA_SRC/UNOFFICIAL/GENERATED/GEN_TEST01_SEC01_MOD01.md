## 📝 Detailed Explanations

---

### Q1 · Words in Context · ✅ Answer: B — *flourish*

**Passage summary:** Hopi corn is uniquely suited to arid conditions, sending roots six feet deep in sandy soil with minimal rainfall.

**Why B is correct:** "Flourish" means to grow or develop successfully. The passage describes corn that doesn't just survive but is "uniquely suited" to harsh conditions — it actively thrives by sending deep roots. "Flourish" captures this positive, active success.

> [!tip] Key Strategy: Definition Clue
> The surrounding context — "uniquely suited," roots reaching "six feet" — describes active, robust growth, not mere survival. Look for the word that matches the *degree* of success described.

**Why others fail:**
- **A (function):** Too mechanical and vague — machines function; plants flourish.
- **C (persist):** Implies barely surviving. The passage describes thriving, not struggling.
- **D (endure):** Like "persist," implies hardship and survival, not success.

> [!danger] Trap: Persist (C) / Endure (D)
> Both are "survival" words — tempting because the passage mentions harsh conditions. But the context emphasizes *success* in those conditions, not mere survival. The "uniquely suited" clue points to flourishing, not enduring.

### Q2 · Words in Context · ✅ Answer: C — *understated*

**Passage summary:** Although the public celebrates large-scale grandeur, Bo Bardi was drawn to the opposite: community centers, repurposed ruins, neglected lots.

**Why C is correct:** "Although" signals a contrast. The public loves "visual grandeur" (large, impressive). Bo Bardi was drawn to the ________: small, humble, unglamorous spaces. "Understated" = modest, not showy — the precise opposite of grandeur.

> [!tip] Key Strategy: Contrast Clue ("Although")
> The blank must oppose "visual grandeur." The examples (community centers, repurposed ruins, neglected lots) are all modest, humble spaces. "Understated" is the precise match.

**Why others fail:**
- **A (impractical):** Nothing in the passage suggests impracticality.
- **B (grandiose):** This is the word being contrasted WITH — it's the opposite of what Bo Bardi chose.
- **D (inaccessible):** Community centers and gathering spaces are explicitly accessible.
